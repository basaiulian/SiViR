1. Intro

SiViR(Similar Video Retriever) is a Web Application that offers you the possibility of searching videos on multiple platforms such as: YouTube, Vimeo, Instagram.

2. What can you do on our website?

2.1 Similar Video Retriever

Helps you to avoid wasting time browsing different platforms to find similar videos.

By introducing your keyword(s) in the search bar and selecting your filter options (order, duration and a specific source) our website will give you the results you would have received by searching on each platform separately.

By introducing the URL of a video in the search bar will allow you to get videos similar to the one you are looking for.

2.2 Your Feed

If you have created an account and you are logged in, we will create a feed based on your searches. You will have access to videos either directly on the page, or through an RSS feed, or you can download them in CSV format.
